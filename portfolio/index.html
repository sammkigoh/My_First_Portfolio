Introduction

Samm Kigo is a Product Marketing Manager at Usiku Games, a gaming-for-good social impact game development studio based in Nairobi, Kenya. He is passionate about using technology for social impact, specifically designing and developing solutions that solve Africans' unique problems. Samuel has extensive experience in digital marketing, having worked for a digital marketing agency and startups in Kenya where he has generated users/customers and created communities for various brands and companies. He’s currently taking courses in UI/UX Design and Software Engineering. Samuel wants to understand the problems that Africans face and work to design and develop innovative tech solutions.
